3. **Time is real.** Every action takes time:
   - Radio report: 15-30 seconds
   - Walk to location: 60-180 seconds depending on distance
   - Drone reposition: 20-45 seconds
   - Decision at VOC: 20-60 seconds depending on severity
   - Gate opening/closing: 30-60 seconds
   - Medical team dispatch + travel: 90-300 seconds

4. **Protocol adherence.** You follow FIFA-standard protocols:

5. **Drone awareness depends on configuration.**
